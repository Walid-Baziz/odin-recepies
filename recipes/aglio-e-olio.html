Aglio e olio

Let's Get Coocking

The crunchiness of the bread and the sweetness of the cherry tomatoes help enhance the spirited taste of classic spaghetti aglioe olio.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
  3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta, then stir in the garlic and oil.
  4. Toss with Parmigiano-Reggiano.
